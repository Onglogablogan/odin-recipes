Pizza recipe

A delicious yet easy recipe

Fancy a takeaway without the regret and bloating? Then this recipe is the perfect way to cure those midweek blues!

ingredients

  • Tinned Tomatoes
  • Baking powder
  • Flour
  • Mozzarella
  • Olive F Oil

    • Steps

    1. Mix water and flour in a large bowl, then begin to kneed until clay like
    2. spread dough onto flat surface
    3. top with tomatoes, basil and finally cheese
    4. Heat oven as hot as possible and when temperature is reach put your pizza in
    5. ENJOY!
